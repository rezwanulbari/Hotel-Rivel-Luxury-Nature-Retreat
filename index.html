The Rivel Difference

Hotel Rivel is a non-profit, Costa Rican-owned luxury nature retreat nestled in the heart of Turrialba's pristine cloud forest. Our commitment to sustainable tourism and environmental conservation creates an authentic experience that connects guests with the natural beauty of Costa Rica while supporting local communities.

From our infinity pool overlooking the Turrialba Valley to our farm-to-table restaurant featuring organic ingredients grown on-site, every detail is designed to immerse you in the pure essence of Pura Vida while maintaining the highest standards of luxury and comfort.

Farm-to-Table Culinary Experience

Our award-winning restaurant showcases the finest Costa Rican flavors using ingredients grown in our organic gardens and sourced from local farmers. Experience authentic Pura Vida cuisine with a modern twist, paired with breathtaking views of the Turrialba Valley.

From freshly roasted coffee beans to tropical fruits picked at sunrise, every dish tells the story of Costa Rica's rich agricultural heritage and our commitment to sustainable gastronomy.
